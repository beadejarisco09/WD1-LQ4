iPhone 11

iPhone 11 is a smartphone with a 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD display, the A13 Bionic chip, and a dual 12MP camera system (Wide and Ultra Wide).

Camera

The iPhone 11 features a dual 12MP rear camera system with wide and ultra-wide lenses, supporting features like Night mode, Portrait mode, and 4K video recording. Key hardware includes a wide ƒ/1.8 aperture lens and an ultra-wide ƒ/2.4 aperture lens with a 120° field of view and 2x optical zoom out. It also boasts software advancements like Deep Fusion, Smart HDR, and a QuickTake video recording feature.

Performance

The iPhone 11's performance is good for everyday tasks, powered by the A13 Bionic chip, though multitasking can be impacted by its 4GB of RAM. It handles casual use like browsing and texting smoothly, with respectable single-core performance. While it can run graphically demanding games, it may require lower settings and cause the device to warm up, especially during extended use.

Battery

The iPhone 11 has a 3,110 mAh (11.91 Wh) non-removable lithium-ion battery. It supports wired fast charging (with PD 2.0), reaching 50% in 30 minutes, and wireless charging (Qi). In terms of usage, Apple states it can last up to 17 hours of video playback, 10 hours of streamed video playback, or 65 hours of audio playback.
